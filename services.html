Frequently Asked Questions

The stuff you're probably wondering about.

How long will my carpets take to dry?

Typically 4-6 hours. It depends on airflow, humidity, and carpet thickness. Using fans or air conditioning helps speed it up.

Do I need to move all my furniture?

We ask that you move small items, breakables, and electronics. We can usually clean around large items like beds and entertainment units.

Are your cleaning products safe for my kids and pets?

Absolutely. We use high-quality, professional-grade products that are safe and non-toxic once dry. Your family's health is our top priority.

Can you guarantee you'll get every stain out?

No cleaner can honestly promise that. Some substances can permanently dye carpet fibres. We do guarantee we have the best training and products to give it the best possible shot.
