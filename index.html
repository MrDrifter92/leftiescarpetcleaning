Tips & Tricks of the Trade

A bit of free advice from your local expert.

Mistake #1: Scrubbing Stains

Scrubbing a fresh spill is the worst thing you can do. It pushes the stain deeper into the carpet fibres and can damage them permanently. Always blot gently with a clean, white cloth, working from the outside in.

Mistake #2: Supermarket Sprays

Those "miracle" sprays often contain bleaching agents that can discolour your carpet or harsh detergents that leave a sticky residue, attracting even more dirt over time. Most of the time, they just make our job harder.

Mistake #3: Waiting Too Long

The longer a stain sits, the harder it is to remove. Acidic stains from coffee or pet urine can permanently dye carpet fibres. For the best chance of a complete removal, give us a call sooner rather than later.
